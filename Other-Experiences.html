I was Member of the founding team of the Hult Prize in the Campus of Bizerte.
The Hult Prize challenges young people around the world to solve the planet's most pressing issues through social entrepreneurship.
The OnCampus program serves as the first phase of the annual Hult Prize competition. We organized training programs and community events at the universities.
The goal of every OnCampus program is to select one winning team, who will represent their university in the Regional Summits.
